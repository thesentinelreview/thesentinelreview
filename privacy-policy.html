The Sentinel Review is published by Sentinel Media Group, LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate the website at thesentinelreview.com and related services including the daily briefing newsletter and the Intel Dashboard.

When you subscribe to our newsletter or create an account, we collect your name, email address, and optional organization affiliation. We do not collect payment card details directly — all payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. under their own privacy policy.

We use your email address solely to send you the daily briefing and any transactional emails related to your subscription. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Our newsletter is delivered via Buttondown (buttondown.com). By subscribing you agree to Buttondown's Terms of Service. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

We do not use tracking cookies. Third-party services embedded in our site (Cloudflare, Stripe) may set cookies in accordance with their own policies.

6. Data Retention

Subscriber records are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security purposes.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing contact@thesentinelreview.com. We will respond within 30 days.
